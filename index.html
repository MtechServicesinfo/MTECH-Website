About MTech Services

Engineering Vision.
Built to Last.

MTech Services is a freelance 3D CAD and design firm specializing in precision modeling for automotive, off-road, and industrial clients. Every project begins with a problem worth solving and ends with a model ready to build.

Using SolidWorks as our primary design platform, we deliver production-ready models, manufacturing drawings, and full assemblies — from a single bracket to an entire chassis. Whether you're a fabricator, engineer, or inventor, we translate your concept into geometry that works.

Based in Southern California, we serve clients across motorsports, off-road, commercial fabrication, and prototype development. Let's bring your ideas to life.
